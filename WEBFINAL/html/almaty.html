About Almaty

Almaty is Kazakhstan's largest city and was once the capital. It is known for its vibrant cultural life, beautiful mountain backdrop, and rich history.

Attractions

Ile-Alatau National Park

A popular destination for hiking and sightseeing in the mountains.

Central State Museum

The largest museum in Almaty, showcasing Kazakhstan's history and culture.

Medeo Ice Rink

Famous high-altitude ice skating rink located in the mountains.
