About Astana

Astana, now known as Nur-Sultan, is the capital of Kazakhstan. Located in the center of the country, it is a political, cultural, and economic hub, known for its modern architecture and rapid development.

Attractions

Baiterek

A famous monument symbolizing a Kazakh legend about the tree of life.

Nur Astana Mosque

One of the largest mosques in Central Asia, located at the heart of the city.

Ak Orda

The official residence of the President of Kazakhstan, a symbol of statehood.
